Welcome to the Rain Game!

Hello! Meet the creators of the Rain Game: Sharon Cai, Shyla Grace, Kirstin Gregoire, and Leeah Michael. The thought behind this game comes from an idea that Leeah had while brainstorming projects for the end of the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at Amazon (Seattle). Together, the four wanted to collaborate and make the game reality. They worked together with the knowledge and experience they gained through the summer of GWC. The Rain Game is an interactive game programmed using Python. This site was made with HTML and CSS.
